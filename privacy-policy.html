Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

In short: LifeSignal uses anonymous authentication. We don't require your email, phone number, or any personal information to use the app. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. We don't track you.

1. Who We Are

LifeSignal ("the App") is developed and operated by Nikolay Matorin, an independent developer based in Germany. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the LifeSignal iOS app.

Contact:
Nikolay Matorin
Email: lifesignal.app@gmail.com

2. Authentication

LifeSignal uses anonymous authentication provided by Firebase Authentication. When you first launch the app, an anonymous account is created automatically. This means:

  • No email address is required
  • No password is required
  • No phone number is required
  • No social media login is required

Your account is identified only by a randomly generated User ID (UID) that cannot be traced back to your real identity.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the app to function. Here is a complete list:

3.1 Data You Provide

Data Required? Purpose
Display Name Optional Shown to your observers so they can identify you
Observer Name Optional Your reference label for an observer you add
Observer Email Optional Your reference info for an observer you add
Observer Phone Optional Your reference info for an observer you add
Emergency Contact Optional Stored in your settings for quick access to your emergency contact

3.2 Data Generated by App Usage

Data Purpose
Check-in timestamps Record when you check in (core app functionality)
Streak and progress data Track your check-in consistency
User role (Subject/Observer) Determine which features to show you
Reminder time preference Schedule your daily check-in reminder
In-app purchase history Record voluntary tip jar contributions

3.3 Technical Data

Data Purpose
Anonymous User ID Identify your account (Firebase Authentication)
Device Token (FCM) Deliver push notifications to your device
Crash reports Identify and fix app crashes (via Sentry)
Performance data Monitor app performance (via Sentry)

4. How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively for app functionality:

  • Processing your daily check-ins and maintaining streaks
  • Sending push notifications to your observers when you miss a check-in
  • Delivering reminder notifications at your preferred time
  • Processing tip jar purchases via Apple's StoreKit
  • Diagnosing crashes and improving app stability

We do NOT use your data for: advertising, marketing, analytics, profiling, data brokerage, or any form of tracking across apps or websites.

5. Third-Party Services

LifeSignal uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Database for storing check-in data, user profiles, and observer relationships
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Push notification delivery
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Server-side logic for missed check-in detection and observer notifications

Firebase data is stored in the EUR3 (Europe) region. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Sentry

  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Collects crash logs, stack traces, and device information
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to Sentry

Sentry's privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy

Apple StoreKit

  • Processes in-app purchases (Tip Jar)
  • Payment information is handled entirely by Apple — we never see or store your payment details

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore in the EUR3 (Europe) region. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to the database is protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure users can only read and write their own data.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your anonymous account exists. Since there is no login mechanism, your account persists on your device until you either:

  • Uninstall the app (your anonymous UID is lost, though server-side data persists until manually cleaned)
  • Request data deletion (see Section 9)

8. Children's Privacy

LifeSignal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of all your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, send an email to lifesignal.app@gmail.com with your request. Since we use anonymous authentication, we may ask you to provide your anonymous User ID (available in the app's Settings screen) to locate your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. For significant changes, we will notify users through the app.
