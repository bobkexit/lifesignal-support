## About LifeSignal

LifeSignal is a free daily wellness check-in app that creates a real safety net between you and the people who care about you. Your observers receive real-time push notifications if you miss a check-in.

- Completely free — no subscriptions
- Anonymous authentication — no email or personal data required
- Real-time push notifications to observers
- Streak tracking and daily reflections
- Emergency crisis hotline integration
- German and English language support
